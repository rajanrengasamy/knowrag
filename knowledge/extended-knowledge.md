## Essential Academic Papers & Research

### Foundational Papers

43. **"The Superinvestors of Graham-and-Doddsville"** - Warren Buffett (1984)
    - Columbia Business School speech
    - Evidence against EMH

44. **"Value vs. Glamour: A Global Phenomenon"** - Fama & French (1998)
    - Value premium documentation
    - International evidence

45. **"The Cross-Section of Expected Stock Returns"** - Fama & French (1992)
    - Three-factor model
    - Size and value effects

46. **"Contrarian Investment, Extrapolation, and Risk"** - Lakonishok, Shleifer & Vishny (1994)
    - Value investing explained by behavioral biases
    - Not just risk premium

47. **"Quality Minus Junk"** - Asness, Frazzini & Pedersen (2013)
    - Quality factor definition
    - Combining quality with value

48. **"Value and Momentum Everywhere"** - Asness, Moskowitz & Pedersen (2013)
    - Global factor evidence
    - Cross-asset class strategies

49. **"Buffett's Alpha"** - Frazzini, Kabiller & Pedersen (2013)
    - Decomposing Buffett's returns
    - Quality + leverage + long-term focus

50. **"The Illusion of Growth"** - Holt CFROI methodology papers
    - Economic returns vs accounting returns
    - Fade rates and competitive dynamics

### Accounting & Financial Statement Analysis

51. **"The Quality of Accruals and Earnings"** - Sloan (1996)
    - Accrual anomaly
    - Earnings quality signals

52. **"Financial Statement Analysis and the Prediction of Stock Returns"** - Ou & Penman (1989)
    - Fundamental signals
    - Predictive financial ratios

53. **"Accounting Anomalies and Fundamental Analysis"** - Piotroski (2000)
    - F-Score methodology
    - High B/M stock selection

54. **"Business Strategy, Overvaluation, and Stock Price Crashes"** - Habib & Hasan (2017)
    - Competitive position and risk
    - Overvaluation detection

### Behavioral Finance

55. **"Prospect Theory: An Analysis of Decision Under Risk"** - Kahneman & Tversky (1979)
    - Foundation of behavioral finance
    - Loss aversion and reference points

56. **"Investor Psychology and Security Market Under- and Overreactions"** - Daniel, Hirshleifer & Subrahmanyam (1998)
    - Overconfidence and biased self-attribution
    - Momentum and reversals

57. **"A Survey of Behavioral Finance"** - Barberis & Thaler (2003)
    - Comprehensive overview
    - Limits to arbitrage

### Market Efficiency Debates

58. **"The Efficient Market Hypothesis: Review of Theory and Empirical Work"** - Fama (1970)
    - Original EMH formulation
    - Know what you're arguing against

59. **"Anomalies: The January Effect"** - Haugen & Lakonishok (1987)
    - Calendar anomalies
    - Market inefficiencies

60. **"The New Finance: The Case Against Efficient Markets"** - Robert Haugen (1995)
    - Critique of EMH
    - Overreaction hypothesis
